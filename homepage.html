The Greenprint of the oul.

Explore our literary analysis, historical analysis and works cited page.

Essential Question

How do writers use the natural world to reflect or shape human emotion, imagination, or moral understanding?

Thesis

Woodsworth grew up surrounded by nature and, after suffering grief at a young age, found emotional comfort by spending time outdoors. When he was inside and feeling down, his memories of nature lightened his mood, giving him his appreciation for finding joy in nature.
